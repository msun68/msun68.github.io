My First Century Ride - Mount Hamilton Loop

Posted by Michael Sun Blog on Saturday, April 20, 2013

How time flies! It has been almost a year since I started cycling. Last year(2012), I have accomplished several long rides(100k). Since then I have always been thinking to chanllenge myself to do a century, a 100-mile long ride.

However, it is hard to find a 100-mile route in the Bay Area that I can do it by myself alone.

Several months ago, a cycling friend hosted a event to visit San Antonio Valley wildflowers in this Spring(info). I finally had a chance to accomplish this milestone.

The route: Milpitas>Mt.Hamilton>The Junction>Livermore>Sunol>Milpitas (distance: 101.5mi, elevation: 10561ft, map)
